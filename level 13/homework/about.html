about Goal-oriented academy







In goal-oriented academy, such events as goa_land, hakaton, are held. These events are designed for the student to demonstrate his skills, as well as to improve group work and relationships with other people. And most importantly, writing codes is a labor of love, a discipline Right and right thinking, these are all things that are taught in the goal-oriented academ

  1. Goa_land

Goa_land is an event where students get to know their group members, mentors, leaders. Entertainment events such as volleyball, tug-of-war and nail driving are held here.





  1. Hakaton

A hackathon is a meeting of various Goa groups where various competitions are held, such as coding and hacking. The winning team from both will receive a prize. Also, a leader who studies best is appointed in the teams, an interview is recorded with the successful student.
